Ch 1. Scope of law

§1 This law is meant to mitigate the effects of radition on health and life in case of some sort of accident regarding nuclear energy or other radioactive materials.

§2 This law may be used to prosecute actions before its passing according to Criminal Code ch 1. §5

Ch 2. Obligattions

§1 You may not build a reactor or build something that has a possibilty of creating deadly radiation within 150 blocks of where people reguraly are.

§2 When building anything that can cause deadly radition you must notify the goverment and all involved players of the placement of such a structure and any risks that structure can create.

§3 Creating nuclear bombs is not allowed.

Ch 3. Punishment for not following obligattions

§1 Any possible punishment in the Criminal Code may be used as punishment. A fine is also allowable punishment. It has to be in proportion to the magnitude of their recklessness.
