The Constitution

ch 1. Basic rights

§1 All power comes from the citizens: Eco, TwoThirds, Herman, and the gronglets.

§2 Everyone is equal under the law and is entitled to Freedom of speech.

ch 2. parliament

§1 Parliament has power to write, changes, and abolish laws.

§2 Only using law can parliament use their power.

§3 Changes to the constitution require unanimity of our 3 citizens.

§4 For enacting law only a majority is required.

§5 A speaker shall be appointed by a majority of parliament and shall have the power to assemble parliament and will lead procedures.

§6 Any law cannot discriminant against any player or players or grongles or grongles.

§7 Votes in parliament are public.

ch 3. Courts

§1 The court is used for disputes of the law.

§2 The judge is the third player that is not the defendant or prosecutor.

The decision shall be a coin flip in case a judge that does not have a conflict of interest cannot be appointed.

§3 In the case where coin flips are used the third player is the speaker of the court if they want to.
