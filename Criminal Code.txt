Criminal Code

ch 1. Nature of Crimes 

Section 1 Crimes must be committed with intent.
Section 2 Courts shall decide whether or not a crime has been committed and give sentences.
Section 3 A crime is not a crime if a consent form is signed by the person the crime is committed against before the crime took place.
Section 4 The death penalty should be an allowable punishment for all crimes.
Section 5 You may be convicted of a crime that was not a crime at the time when the act was made if it was later added by parliament.

ch 2. Crimes

Section 1 1st 2nd 3rd degree Murder is a crime, and the punishment is death.
Section 2 Theft is when someone takes something from someone else without their permission. The punishment is returning what you stole, and a mark in a public record.  
Section 3 No kidnaping the gronglets, you may move them. The punishment is a ban for a week.
Section 4 If someone changes property without the owners permission they shall be convicted of property damage. The punishment is 1 detonated TNT on the property of the convicted.
Section 5 If someone lies in court they shall be convicted of perjury. The punishment is that the convicted can only eat dried kelp for between 2-72 hours. 
Section 6 If someone does an act that sabotages or contradicts the state they have committed treason. The punishment is banishment for between 1 and 31 days. 
Section 7 If someone uses a gronglet as slingshot ammo or in another way endangered a gronglet they shall be convicted of gronglet endangerment. The punishment is a 730 Minecraft days ban.
